Our Instrument

See what makes our instrument truly one of a kind.

What makes up the all-in-one?

Our instrument has a variety of injection sites for both liquid and gas compatible samples, GC and LC columns, a vaporizer/condenser that allows for compatibility between GC and LC column eluents and their corresponding ion sources, a triple quadrupole, Orbitrap, TOF mass analyzer system, and three different detectors.

Injection Sites and Columns

Our instrument has three options for injection sites. We have the traditional GC and LC injection ports with corresponding autosamplers, as well as a direct-infusion syringe for samples that do not require separation. We also have a GC column and LC column for adequate separation of samples. The eluent from columns or direct-infusion syringe can be ran through the vaporizer/condenser unit so all samples are compatible with all available ion sources.
*Specialized columns sold separately.

Ion Sources

This instrument is equipped with the traditional EI, CI, APCI, ESI and nano-ESI ion sources. These allow for ionization of all sample types you may encounter.
*MALDI ion source attachment available for additional fee.

Mass Analyzers and Detectors

The mass analyzers included are: triple quadrupole, Orbitrap, and time-of-flight. All mass analyzers have the ability to be turned on or off, allowing for a wide variety of combinations to meet your needs. Due to the ability of these mass analyzers to turn on or off, you have the choice for a continuous or batch-mode analysis. The detectors included are: electron multiplier, photomultiplier tube, and charge coupled detector. You may choose to use one or all detectors at once.
*Signal to noise ratio suffers when using more than one detector.
